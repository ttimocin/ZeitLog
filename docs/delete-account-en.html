How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account from within the ZeitLog app:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings page
  3. Click "Delete Account" button
  4. Type "DELETE" to confirm
  5. Confirm the action

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently deleted:

  • All work records (check-in/check-out times)
  • Cloud backup data (Firebase)
  • Local storage data (data on your device)
  • User account information

Important Notes

  • This action is irreversible
  • All your data will be permanently deleted
  • If you want to backup your data, export it as CSV first
  • You must be logged in to the app to delete your account
